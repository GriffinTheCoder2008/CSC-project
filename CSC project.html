 The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is an American media franchise and shared
 universe centered on a series of superhero films, independently produced by Marvel Studios
 and based on characters that appear in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. 
 The franchise includes comic books, short films, television series, and digital series. 
 The shared universe, much like the original Marvel Universe in comic books, was established by
 crossing over common plot elements, settings, cast, and characters.
 
 Some of the charcters in the MCU are Iron Man, Thor, The Hulk, Black Widow and 
 Hawkeye(Now known as RONAN).
